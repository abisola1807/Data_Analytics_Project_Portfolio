About this project

AfriTech Electronics, a consumer electronics company specializing in smartphones, tablets, and wearables, faced a surge in negative social media sentiment, product recalls, and customer complaints that began eroding customer trust and market share. The goal of this project was to transform these challenges into opportunities through data-driven monitoring and crisis management.

Objectives:

Track and analyze customer sentiment across social media platforms.

Identify crisis patterns related to product reliability, complaints, and recalls.

Improve response times and customer satisfaction through data insights.

Support brand reputation recovery using visual analytics and KPIs.
